sign_language.features.landmarks module

Hand landmark feature extraction.

Normalises raw MediaPipe hand landmarks and engineers geometric features (joint angles, fingertip distances) to match the training pipeline used by the Landmark MLP.

sign_language.features.landmarks.normalize_landmarks(landmarks: list[dict]) → numpy.ndarray[source]

Centre landmarks on the wrist and scale by the wrist-to-middle-MCP distance.

Translates all 21 landmarks so that landmark 0 (wrist) is at the origin, then divides by the Euclidean norm of landmark 9 (middle-finger MCP) to make the representation scale-invariant.

Parameters:

landmarks – List of 21 dicts each with x, y, and z keys containing normalised MediaPipe coordinates.

Returns:

A (21, 3) float32 numpy.ndarray of normalised landmark coordinates.

sign_language.features.landmarks.extract_landmark_features(landmarks: list[dict]) → torch.Tensor[source]

Build the full feature vector from raw MediaPipe landmarks.

Concatenates three feature groups into a single 1D tensor:

  1. Flattened normalised landmark coordinates (21 × 3 = 63 values).

  2. Joint angles for each triplet in JOINT_TRIPLETS.

  3. Euclidean distances between all fingertip pairs in FINGERTIP_PAIRS.

The resulting feature vector must match the input dimensionality expected by the Landmark MLP exactly.

Parameters:

landmarks – List of 21 landmark dicts with x, y, and z keys, as returned by MediaPipe or the preprocessing pipeline.

Returns:

A torch.Tensor of shape (1, feature_dim) with dtype float32, ready for direct input to the Landmark MLP.
